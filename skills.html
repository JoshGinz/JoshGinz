Computational Analysis and Design Proficiency

My journey through computational analysis and design has been marked by a deep engagement with Python and MATLAB, languages that are the bedrock of my problem-solving approach. Additive Manufacturing and Equipment Maintenance

In the realm of additive manufacturing, I’ve not only strived to push the boundaries of what can be created but also to deeply understand the intricacies of the technology itself. My engagement extends beyond mere operation — encompassing the maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair of 3D printers. This holistic approach ensures not just the functionality but the optimization of the printing process, as I work to maintain the delicate balance between machine efficiency and the high fidelity of printed components.
